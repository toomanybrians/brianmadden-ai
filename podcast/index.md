# Citrix AI Hotsheet

Brian's podcast, co-hosted with Dave Brear (account technology strategist, Citrix). Monthly, ~45 minutes per episode. The show is for IT leaders, architects, and practitioners responsible for bringing AI into real enterprise environments at scale.

Brian and Dave come at every topic from two angles: Brian focuses on where AI is heading and what it means for the future of enterprise work; Dave focuses on what actually works inside complex enterprise environments today.

The show covers what's actually changing for enterprise AI: agentic systems and computer use, identity and governance for non-human personas, the economics of AI at scale, and what it takes to bring AI into environments that can't be ripped out and replaced.
